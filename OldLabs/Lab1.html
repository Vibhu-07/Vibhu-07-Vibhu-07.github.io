City Information

  • State: Punjab
  • Country: India
  • Population: 862,886
  • Mayor: Jagdish Raja

The Sports City of India is Jalandhar. The region around Jalandhar has a long history of study and education. It is one of the oldest cities, having historic buildings that are still standing and the earliest temples ever built by humans.

One of the top destinations to see in Jalandhar is the oldest Hindu temple,"Devi Talab Mandir",which has a gold-plated structure.
